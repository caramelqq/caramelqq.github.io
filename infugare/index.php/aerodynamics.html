Aerodynamics

After numerous iterations, the airfoil used in the final design was determined to be the INF-1, a custom designed airfoil for the Valens NRG.  This wing will have a 40% taper ratio to obtain an elliptical distribution.  Flaps  are implemented to allow for  increased lift at take-off.
